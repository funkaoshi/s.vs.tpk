The [original DCC RPG zine][1] is back, bigger than ever. (Literally.) You can help Dak Ultimak publish [a new edition of Crawl's first issue][2], with an alternate cover by Doug Kovacs. Long time readers may recall that [Crawl! was the first thing I wrote about on this blog][3]. I was lucky enough to receive one of the limited edition black-on-black versions of the zine. Maybe one day I can sell it and buy a Kia.
